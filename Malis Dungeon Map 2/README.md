# Diablo-Style Dungeon Map Viewer

Welcome to the Diablo-Style Dungeon Map Viewer! This tool provides an immersive and customizable experience for exploring dungeon maps. As soon as you enter a "dungeon," the map loads instantly, offering a range of features to enhance your gameplay.

## Features

### Configurable Colors
- Personalize your map with a variety of color options.
- Easily distinguish different map elements by assigning unique colors.

### Adjustable Thickness
- Modify the thickness of map lines for better visibility and clarity.
- Tailor the map's appearance to your preference.

### Mission Objective Rewards
- Automatically display mission objective rewards when you are within range.
- Plan your path efficiently to maximize your blitzing speed.

### Elevator Locations
- Highlight elevator locations on the map when you are nearby.
- Quickly navigate between different levels of the dungeon.

### Door Locations
- Draw all doors (no visibility limit)

### Zone Exclusion
- Customize which zones are not loaded on the map.
- To add a new id simply shift+enter in the text view to go to the next line
